# mystsh User Journeys

This document outlines the end-to-end experience for our two primary personas: the **Parent** (Decision Maker) and the **Teen/Youth** (Primary User).

---

## 1. Persona: The Parent (Maria, 42)
**Goal:** Provide her teen with financial freedom while maintaining oversight and teaching responsibility.

### Journey 1.1: Onboarding & Funding
1. **Discovery:** Maria downloads the mystsh app after hearing it works with her existing **Wise** account.
2. **Account Creation:** She signs up using MFA and completes a quick identity verification.
3. **Linking Source:** Maria connects her Wise card. mystsh uses secure tokenization to establish the link without storing her full card details.
4. **Teen Invite:** She enters her son Alex’s phone number to send a secure invite link.

### Journey 1.2: Setting Guardrails
1. **Limit Setting:** Maria sets a weekly spending limit of $50 for Alex.
2. **Category Control:** She toggles "Gaming" to OFF, deciding Alex should focus on his studies this week.
3. **Real-time Monitoring:** Alex buys a sandwich; Maria receives an instant notification: *"Alex spent $8.50 at Subway. Remaining limit: $41.50."*
4. **Emergency Action:** Alex loses his phone at the park. Maria opens the app and taps **"FREEZE CARD"** instantly.

---

## 2. Persona: The Teen/Youth (Alex, 15)
**Goal:** Use digital payments like his friends and earn rewards for smart money habits.

### Journey 2.1: First Setup & "Tap to Pay"
1. **Acceptance:** Alex receives a text from his mom, downloads mystsh, and creates his profile.
2. **Card Provisioning:** He sees a virtual "mystsh" card waiting for him. He taps **"Add to Apple Wallet."**
3. **The First Purchase:** At a local cafe, Alex taps his iPhone on the terminal. The payment is successful.
4. **Instant Feedback:** He checks the mystsh app immediately to see his new balance and the "Cafe" logo in his transaction history.

### Journey 2.2: Gamified Literacy & Rewards
1. **Quest Discovery:** Alex notices he’s only 200 points away from a "$10 Amazon Gift Card."
2. **Learning:** He starts the **"Magic of Compounding"** quest—a 2-minute interactive module with a quiz at the end.
3. **Earning:** He passes the quiz and hears a satisfying "cha-ching" sound as 50 **Stash Points** are added to his profile.
4. **Goal Setting:** Alex creates a "New Sneakers" savings goal for $120. He allocates 10% of his next allowance to this goal automatically within the app.

---

## 3. The "Financial Handshake" (Cross-Persona)
*This journey describes the interaction between the two personas.*

1. **Request:** Alex is at the cinema and realizes the ticket is more than his remaining balance. He taps **"Request Extra Funds"** in the app.
2. **Approval:** Maria receives a notification: *"Alex is requesting $15.00 for 'Entertainment'."* She taps **Approve**.
3. **Execution:** The funds are instantly available in Alex's digital wallet, and he buys his ticket.
4. **Audit:** Both see the transaction in their respective feeds, maintaining transparency and trust.
